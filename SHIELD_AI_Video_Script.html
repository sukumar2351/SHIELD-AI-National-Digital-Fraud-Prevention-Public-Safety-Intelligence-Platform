Screen Recording & Export Guide

Step-by-Step Recording Instructions

Step 1 — Open the Demo in Chrome

Double-click SHIELD_AI_Demo_Video.html to open it in Google Chrome. Press F to enter fullscreen. Press F5 to reload if needed. The demo will auto-play through all 10 scenes.

Step 2 — Configure OBS Studio
  • Download OBS from obsproject.com
  • Add a Display Capture or Window Capture source targeting Chrome
  • Set Output: 1920×1080 @ 60fps
  • Format: MP4 | Encoder: x264 | Quality: CRF 18
  • Audio: Add a separate Microphone input for voice-over
Step 3 — Record with Keyboard Controls
  • Press SPACE to pause auto-advance during recording if you need extra time
  • Press → (right arrow) to advance to the next scene manually
  • Press ← (left arrow) to go back to any scene
  • Each scene auto-advances: 7–8 seconds per scene
Step 4 — Record Voice-Over Separately
  • Use Audacity (free) or OBS audio track for voice-over recording
  • OR use ElevenLabs AI voice generator (elevenlabs.io) for professional AI narration
  • Paste each scene's voice-over text into ElevenLabs — select "Rachel" or "Antoni" voice
  • Export each scene as separate MP3, then mix in DaVinci Resolve
Step 5 — Edit in DaVinci Resolve (Free)
  • Import screen recording + voice-over audio + background music
  • Sync voice-over to corresponding scenes using markers
  • Apply Color Grading: Increase blue/cyan channel, deepen shadows
  • Add HUD overlays: Corner brackets, scanlines (DaVinci Resolve templates)
  • Layer music: Lower volume during voice-over, raise during transitions
  • Add captions/subtitles from voice-over script (VEED.io auto-captions)
Step 6 — Export Final Video
  • Format: MP4 (H.264)
  • Resolution: 1920×1080
  • Frame rate: 60fps
  • Bitrate: 15–20 Mbps (high quality)
  • Audio: AAC 320kbps
  • File name: SHIELD_AI_Demo_ET_Hackathon2.mp4

Alternative: AI Video Generation (CapCut / Runway)

If you prefer AI-assisted video generation instead of screen recording:

  • CapCut PC (free): Import screenshots from the HTML demo, apply cinematic transitions, add AI voice-over and music automatically
  • Runway ML (runwayml.com): Use "Text to Video" for opening scene — India map with cyber alerts emerging
  • Canva Video Pro: Drag-and-drop each scene screenshot, apply dark transitions, add professional voice-over
  • Invideo AI (invideo.io): Paste the voice-over script + upload screenshots — AI auto-assembles the video
🏆 Pro Tip for Hackathon Judges

Keep the demo video to exactly 3 minutes 30 seconds. Judges typically have limited time. The most impactful moments are: the SHIELD reveal (0:25), the Fraud DNA animation (1:00), and the finale tagline (3:25). Make sure these three moments are visually the strongest.
